BEGO

Studies suggest that people who visualize their goals are 70% more likely to acheive them than those who do not. BEGO is a tool designed for aiding individuals who like to visualize. After entering where you would like to live and what you (would like to) earn, BEGO returns a list of helpful anylitics that will hopefully inspire you to see your goals as something plausable and less of an abstract ideation.
This application is in BETA. The Alpha version will allow you to click on any given city and see the provided anylitics in the middle column.
